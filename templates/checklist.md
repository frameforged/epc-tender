# BID EVALUATION CHECKLIST
## Oil & Gas EPC Construction Tenders — General Template

**Usage:** A copy of this list is created for each incoming bid. Each ☐ is marked ☑ when completed.
**Adaptation:** Sections are updated according to the form list in the tender document.

---

## SECTION A: PRELIMINARY CHECK (ELIMINATION)

If any item in this section FAILs, the bid is not accepted for evaluation.

### A1. Submission Format
- ☐ Bid documents submitted in the required format
- ☐ Commercial and technical sections separated (if required)
- ☐ File naming complies with tender rules
- ☐ Price Schedule available in required formats (Excel + PDF etc.)
- ☐ Submitted before the bid deadline
- ☐ Bid validity period stated and adequate

### A2. Mandatory Documents
_Adapt according to the form list in the tender document (CFB):_

- ☐ Bid letter (signed, sealed)
- ☐ Price summary table (PSSummary)
- ☐ Discipline-based Price Schedule
- ☐ Compliance Declaration
- ☐ Execution Plan
- ☐ Manpower Schedule (workforce histogram)
- ☐ Work schedule (Schedule + S-Curve)
- ☐ _Other project-specific forms: ___________

### A3. Arithmetic Accuracy
- ☐ Unit Price x Quantity = Line Total (on every line)
- ☐ SUM(Line Totals) = Discipline Total
- ☐ SUM(Discipline Totals) = PSSummary Direct Cost (A)
- ☐ Direct (A) + Indirect (B) = Grand Total
- ☐ MH totals consistent (PS <-> PSSummary <-> Manpower Schedule)

**SECTION A RESULT:** ☐ PASSED / ☐ FAILED — Reason: ___________

## SECTION C: TECHNICAL EVALUATION

### C1. Execution Plan
- ☐ Mobilization plan is detailed and realistic
- ☐ Work sequencing is logical (critical path identified)
- ☐ Inter-discipline coordination is planned
- ☐ Precommissioning strategy is clear
- ☐ Quality management approach is defined
- ☐ HSE plan is available and adequate

### C2. Workforce Plan (Manpower)
- ☐ Peak personnel count is realistic
- ☐ Mobilization/demobilization duration is appropriate
- ☐ Ramp-up is gradual and logical
- ☐ Foreman/supervisor ratio is adequate (typical: 1:10-1:15)
- ☐ QA/QC personnel count is adequate
- ☐ HSE personnel count is adequate
- ☐ Expat vs local ratio is stated

### C3. Equipment and Facilities
- ☐ Crane/lifting equipment capacity is adequate
- ☐ Generator capacity is adequate
- ☐ Welding machines / test equipment listed
- ☐ Temporary facilities are planned

### C4. Work Schedule
- ☐ Milestone dates align with client schedule
- ☐ Float/buffer time exists
- ☐ S-Curve is realistic
- ☐ Seasonal efficiency loss is accounted for

### C5. Subcontractors
- ☐ Subcontractor list is provided
- ☐ Experience is stated for critical works

### C6. Technical Deviations
- ☐ Number of technical deviations: _____
- ☐ No deviations that reduce scope
- ☐ Alternative proposals have been technically assessed

**SECTION C RESULT:** ☐ Adequate / ☐ Conditional Acceptance / ☐ Inadequate

### D1. Commercial Deviations
