## PROJECT OVERVIEW

SOCAR is relocating its refinery operations from Baku to Sumgayit in order to reduce environmental pollution. The new refinery:

- Designed to comply with Euro-5 fuel specifications
- Same crude oil quality/quantity as HAOR Refinery Phase 2
- Integration with Azerikimya Steam Cracker Unit (ASCU)
- Two crude oil blends: Light Crude Mix (LCM) and High Sulfur Crude Mix (HSCM)

## TECHNIP ENERGIES PRICE SUMMARY STRUCTURE

The Price Summary (PSSummary) Excel file uses the following structure:

### Discipline Columns (horizontal):
1. CIVIL WORKS
2. PIPING PREFABRICATION WORKS
3. PIPING ERECTION
4. STEEL STRUCTURE ERECTION
5. MECHANICAL ERECTION
6. PAINTING WORKS
7. INSULATION WORKS
8. ELECTRICAL WORKS
9. INSTRUMENT WORKS
10. TELECOM WORKS
11. PRECOMMISSIONING
12. **TOTAL**

### Row Structure:

**(A) DIRECT COSTS:**
- a) Direct labour (including hand tools, consumables, meals)
- b) Direct labour for scaffolding
- c) Direct material (material to be integrated into the facility)
- d) Subcontracted works
- **SUBTOTAL DIRECT COSTS (A)**

**(B) INDIRECT COSTS:**
1. Supervision and indirect labour cost
2. Temporary SITE facilities cost
3. General field expenses of TSF
4. Construction equipment cost (excluding Heavy Lifting)
5. Heavy Lifting Cranes cost
6. Scaffolding cost
7. HSE and Security cost
8. Camp/Accommodation cost (including catering, O&M)
9. Design and engineering cost
10. All local levies, duties, taxes
11. Overheads and profit
- **SUBTOTAL INDIRECT COSTS (B)**

**TOTAL = DIRECT (A) + INDIRECT (B)**

**Important Notes:**
- Scaffolding man-hours and direct cost should be embedded in each item code's productivity and unit price
- Indirect scaffolding cost should be shown as a separate line item
- Yellow cells are to be filled in by the bidder
- Direct costs and manhours totals must match each discipline's Price List total
