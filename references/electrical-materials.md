## Price Note

The above prices are for reference purposes and vary based on:
- Order quantity (10-30% discount on bulk orders)
- Country of origin (30-60% difference between European and Chinese production)
- Certification (IECEx/ATEX certified products are more expensive)
- Lead time (expedited delivery incurs additional cost)
- Freight and customs (15-25% surcharge for Caspian region)
- Currency fluctuations

**Current price research is mandatory for each project.**
